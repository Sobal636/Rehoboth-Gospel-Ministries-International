About

Gospel Ministries International (RGMI) is a Christian organisation that Pr. Unni began praying on June 20, 2012, and registered with the Government of India in December 2021. (Under the Charitable Society Act 1955). For those of us who were born and nurtured in a Hindu household. Due to my child's condition in 2003, I accepted the Lord as my personal saviour and began living as a child of God. As a result, when we comprehend the deliverance that Jesus provided, we can appreciate the Lord's love and the goods of the kingdom of heaven. I was a military officer and then a university of Kerala officer. Since of God's counsel in 2012, and because God's servants are segregated and entrusted with ministry, I've been doing God's work since 2012. Rehoboth Gospel Ministries International was founded as a result of this experience. It is registered with the Government of India, which has received many believers into the Lord and given them the opportunity to inherit the kingdom of heaven, and our ministry has become a source of deliverance for many. Still doing philanthropic work, running the hospital ministries, sponsoring other fasting prayers, inter-ceremony prayers, conventions, Deliverance meetings, conferences, and assisting other pastors.
