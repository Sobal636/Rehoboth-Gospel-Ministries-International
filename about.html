We Live For Passion

It is registered with the Government of India, which has received many believers into the Lord and given them the opportunity to inherit the kingdom of heaven, and our ministry has become a source of deliverance for many. Still doing philanthropic work, running the hospital ministries, sponsoring other fasting prayers, inter-ceremony prayers, conventions, Deliverance meetings, conferences, and assisting other pastors.
