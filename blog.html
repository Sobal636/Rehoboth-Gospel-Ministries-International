Second Coming of Jesus Christ

When will Jesus return?

Jesus will return shortly. No one, however, knows the exact hour when Jesus will return. "But no one knows about that day or hour, not even the angels in heaven, nor the Son, but only the Father," Mark 13:32 states. Interestingly, right before Jesus returned to heaven, the disciples asked Him the same question. Jesus told them at the time that they had no right to know the periods or seasons that were under the Father's power. As a result, Jesus could return tomorrow, next month, next year, or in 100 years. So, how does this affect you and I? Always be prepared for Jesus Christ's second coming.

Why is it taking Jesus so long to return?

Because He is giving humanity as much time as possible to choose and follow Him, Jesus is patiently waiting to return. Jesus desires that as many people as possible repent and join Him in heaven. "But, beloved, remember this one thing: with the Lord, one day is like a thousand years, and a thousand years is as one day," reads 2 Peter 3:8-9. The Lord is not slack in keeping His promise, as some consider slackness, but is patient with us, not wanting anyone to perish but all to come to repentance."

Warnings about the second coming of Jesus?

Many people are confused by a variety of non-biblical views about Christ's second coming. Will He merely appear spiritually to a few people? Will Jesus' second coming take place exclusively in specific geographical locations? Is it possible that Jesus will emerge in the desert? In the last days, Jesus cautioned about deceptions. In fact, Jesus warns followers in Matthew 24:4-5 not to be fooled by those claiming to be Christ in the name of Jesus. As a result, our only safety net is to rely only on the Bible for answers.

Will Jesus come back secretly?

Many folks wonder how many individuals will witness Jesus' return. The second coming of Christ will be spectacular, dazzling, and loud. This event will not be disguised; Jesus will be seen by every human on the planet. He will return, both literally and personally. "Behold, He is coming with clouds, and every eye will see Him," says Revelation 1:7. There will be no one on the planet who is not aware of Jesus' return. Jesus Himself describes the method in which He will appear. According to Matthew 24:27, Christ's return will be like a bolt of lightning illuminating the entire sky from east to west.
